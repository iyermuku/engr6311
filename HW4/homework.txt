Part I. Consider the lateral vibrations of a uniform string that is fixed at both ends. 
Obtain the modal equations when a linear viscous damper is attached to an arbitrary 
point along the string. 

Part II. Consider the lateral vibrations of a uniform beam that is pinned at both ends. 
An external harmonic force is applied at the center of the beam. Use a single-mode 
approximation to obtain the equation for the first mode of the beam. Consider finite 
but small rotations of the beam by including the first nonlinear term when 
approximating the beam curvature.
